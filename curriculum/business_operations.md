# Business Operations Curriculum

## Core Pillars
1. **Business Strategy:** Structured problem-solving, estimation, prioritization, and operational strategy frameworks.
2. **Operational Metrics:** Deep dive into data questions, answering questions about data, and measuring operational health.
3. **Estimation:** Case interview toolkits for market sizing and operational estimation.
4. **Case Interview Toolkits:** Structured playbooks for answering Strategy, Operations, and Estimation cases.
