### 📄 Why a Document over a Sheet?
1. **Long-Form Readability:** Interview transcripts, detailed rubric evaluations, and executive pitches are inherently long-form text. In a Tech Company Sheet, multi-line paragraphs get cramped inside cells, making it painful to scan during revision.
2. **Rich Formatting (Hierarchy & Alerts):** In a Doc, we can use beautiful Markdown formatting—clear headings (`# Question 1`), color-coded callout boxes for critical L4/L5 coaching takeaways, and bolded metric frameworks.
3. **Executive Summary Table:** We can include a clean summary table at the very top of the Doc (Question Title | Domain | Score | Key Takeaway) with clickable links jumping directly to the deep dive below.
