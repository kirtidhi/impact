# Product Operations Rubrics

## Internal Recruiting Expert Breakdown
1. **Problem Solving:** Strategic direction, critical thinking, structured problem-solving, data-driven insights.
2. **Communication:** Executive presence, clear and effective communication.
3. **Business Acumen:** Operations management, technical PA expertise, execution.
4. **Cultural Fit:** Caring about the team, user-first mindset, challenging the status quo.

## L5/L6 Expectations
- **Strategy:** Industry trends, business plans.
- **Operations:** Running org cadences, cross-functional connectors.
- **Leadership:** Partnership with senior leadership to run cross-cutting initiatives.
